Gully G'z - Rock Band from Long Beach, CA

Biography

Rising from the ashes of their old band, Stingray Barbie, Julie Goforth (bass), Matt Lucca (drums), and Patrick Goddard (guitar/vocals), decided to pick up the pieces and continue on with a new project, called Gully G'z, for the post-pandemic era in 2021 and beyond.

Based in Long Beach, CA, Gully G'z brings decades of experience to the table, combining old-school punk, indie, cowpunk and surf, to craft an engaging repertoire of songs.

Former bands the members have been in include Stingray Barbie, Dynamite Gangbusters, Go Pound Salt, Lost Cabras, Plasma Cannon, Cara de Nada and more.
